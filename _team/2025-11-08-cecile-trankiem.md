My research centers on developing robust mathematical and statistical models to characterize infectious disease transmission and inform the design of interventions, by leveraging multiple data streams (epidemiological, pathogen genomic, social contact and mobility data).

Currently, I am developing methods to analyze large genomic datasets, My strategy involves analyzing clusters of identical or nearly identical sequences. By circumventing the need for full phylogenetic tree inference, these methods can analyze datasets orders of magnitude larger than existing tree-based approaches.

My work supported [COVID-19 pandemic response in France](https://modelisation-covid19.pasteur.fr/).
